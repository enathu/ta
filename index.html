Short Profile

When you don't create things, you become defined by your tastes rather than your ability. Your tastes only narrow & exclude people. So create.
- Jonathan Gillette

Hiya! I am Tharique Azeez. I am a visual designer, who works with wide range of design technologies. I am focused on typography, story telling, creative direction, editorial design, visual identity, and graphic design.

I have graduated from the University of East London, England in Technology, and e-Commerce. I have several years of experience in digital and print design, and front-end development.

When I am not working on commissions, I use my time on one of my pet projects, or listening to Radiolab podcasts, which I love.

I also contribute to the TED Open Translation Project, as a Language Coordinator for Tamil.

I run a design studio and type foundry called, Niram Factory. Through that, I release design resources for creative people, and I create typefaces and fonts in Latin and Indic scripts. Tamil Typography is one of my self-initiated projects, which celebrates the unique beauty of Tamil script. You can find my other ongoing projects below. Enjoy!
