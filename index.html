Short Profile

When you don't create things, you become defined by your tastes rather than your ability. Your tastes only narrow & exclude people. So create.

-- Jonathan Gillette

Hiya! I am Tharique Azeez. I am a visual designer, and I work with wide range of design projects and commissions. Focused with typography, story telling, creative direction, editorial design, visual identity, and graphic design. I am glad that I get to work with passionate and interesting clients from around the world.

I have graduated from the University of East London, England in Technology, and e-Commerce. I have several years of experience in digital and print design and front-end development.

When I am not working on commissions, I might be using my time on updating one of my self-initiated side projects. I love listening to Radiolab podcast.

I also, contributing to TED Open Translation Project, as a Language Coordinator for Tamil.

I run a design studio and type foundry called, Niram Factory. With that, I release design resources for creatives, and I design typefaces and develop fonts in Latin and Indic scripts. Tamil Typography is one of my self-initiated projects, which celebrates the unique beauty of Tamil script. You can find my other ongoing self-initiated projects below. Enjoy!
