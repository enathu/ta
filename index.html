When you don't create things, you become defined by your tastes rather than your ability. Your tastes only narrow & exclude people. So create.
- Jonathan Gillette

Words. They unveil a smile or shroud an abyss of sadness. They're the ones you have to utter, be it a heavy storm or innocent sunshine clouded by playful clouds. They are friends, and foes, to whomever dares to live. That's why I am tangled with them, mesmerising computations of the same bare letters everyone sees. I am a typography artist, and a TED language coordinator for Tamil. I create and build bridges, between media environments and people, alike.

Colours and shapes that can convey the unspeakable. Forms that bring into existence the unthinkable. Strokes which consolidate the unabridged. I am a visual designer. I conceive beauty from the faintest idea.

For these, I built a modest home, Niram Labs. That is a lair of creativity and resourcefulness, with its doors open to anybody and anything.
